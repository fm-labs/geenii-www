FAQ

Common questions.

What does "ISO/IEC 42001 compliant" actually mean for GEENii? +

ISO/IEC 42001 is the international AI management system standard published in 2023. For GEENii, compliance means we have certified processes for AI risk assessment, transparency and explainability requirements, human oversight mechanisms, incident response for AI failures, and continual improvement frameworks. We can provide full certification documentation and support your own AI Act compliance assessments.

Can GEENii really run with no internet connectivity? +

Yes. Our air-gapped deployment option runs entirely on-premise with no external network calls. The base models, skill libraries, and all dependencies are packaged for offline installation. Skill Marketplace access is disabled in air-gapped mode, but you can pre-install a curated set of approved skills at deployment time. This option is available for Gov/Defense tier customers.

How does the on-premise deployment work technically? +

GEENii on-premise ships as a set of container images (Docker/Kubernetes) with a Helm chart for configuration. You run it in your own infrastructure — on-prem servers, private cloud, or air-gapped data center. Our team provides deployment support, and enterprise contracts include a dedicated deployment engineer for the initial setup. GPU requirements vary by model size; we provide hardware spec sheets during the procurement process.

What's included in the 99.9% SLA? +

The 99.9% SLA covers API availability for all agent deployments on GEENii Cloud. It includes financial remedies (service credits) for downtime that exceeds the SLA threshold, 24/5 dedicated support with defined response times (P0: 30 min, P1: 2 hr, P2: 8 hr), and regular uptime reporting. On-premise deployments use a VSLA (Verified SLA) model with different terms — contact our team for details.

How does GEENii differ from personal AI assistants? +

Personal AI assistants are excellent for individual workflows and messaging interfaces (WhatsApp, Telegram). GEENii is the enterprise backend — scalable orchestration, compliance, and payment infrastructure. They work well together: a personal AI assistant handles the user-facing interaction layer, while GEENii manages the industrial backend logic. We offer a documented Hybrid Mode that connects the two, giving you the UX flexibility of consumer AI tools with the reliability of GEENii.
